About Faith Church

Our Mission

At Faith Baptist Church, we are dedicated to glorifying God by embracing Biblical truths, sharing the Gospel, and nurturing an authentic Christian community. We help individuals follow Jesus with their whole hearts, discover true fulfillment, and build a deep relationship with Him. Grounded in love for God, compassion for others, and active service, we support disciples on their journey of growth and impactful service. A Welcome From Our Pastor

"Welcome to Faith Church! We are so glad you're here. Our hope is that from the moment you walk through our doors, you'll feel like family. We are a community of imperfect people who have been transformed by the grace of a perfect God, and we want to share that hope with everyone. Whether you're just curious about faith or have been a follower of Jesus for years, there's a place for you here. We can't wait to meet you!"

- Matt Vandeleest, Pastor

Our Beliefs

We hold to the historic Christian faith as revealed in Scripture. Our core beliefs center on the person and work of Jesus Christ, the sovereignty of God, and the power of the Holy Spirit. For a more detailed look at what we believe, please see our full statement of faith.
