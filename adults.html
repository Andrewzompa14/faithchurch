Adult Ministries

Life is Better Together

At Faith Church, we believe you can't do life alone. We are designed for community, and our adult ministries are created to help you build Christ-centered relationships. Whether you're looking for a small group, a Bible study, or just a place to connect, there's a place for you here.

Community Groups

Community Groups are the heart of our ministry. These are small groups of adults who meet regularly in homes to study the Bible, pray for one another, and live out the Christian life together. It's the best way to get connected at Faith Church.

Men's Ministry

Our Men's Ministry is focused on equipping men to be spiritual leaders in their homes, at work, and in the community. We gather for Bible studies, breakfasts, and retreats to sharpen one another and grow in our walk with Christ.

Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry provides opportunities for women of all ages and stages of life to connect, grow in their faith, and encourage one another. Join us for Bible studies, fellowship events, and our annual women's conference.

Ready to Get Connected?

The best way to see what's available is to visit our group finder. Browse different groups and studies to find the perfect fit for you.
