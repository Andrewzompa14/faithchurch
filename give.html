Why 100% Free Giving?

We use Zeffy for our online giving because they are the only major platform that is 100% free for churches. They do not charge any transaction fees, which means every single dollar you donate goes directly to ministry. Zeffy is funded by voluntary contributions from donors, so you may see an option to add a tip to support their platform, but it is completely optional.
