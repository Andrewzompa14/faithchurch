"Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these." - Matthew 19:14

Welcome to Faith Kids! Our mission is to partner with families to lead children into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ. We do this by creating a safe, fun, and engaging environment where kids can learn about the Bible and experience God's love in a way they can understand.

Your First Visit

When you arrive, look for the Faith Kids check-in station in our lower level. One of our friendly volunteers will help you fill out a quick registration form and get your child to the right classroom.

Safety is Our Top Priority

All of our Faith Kids volunteers are background-checked and trained. We use a secure sign-in/out system, and children will only be released to authorized adults. Your child's spiritual, emotional, and physical health is our number one concern.
