Our Ministries

Get Connected

We believe that spiritual growth happens best in community. Our ministries are designed to help you connect with others, grow in your understanding of God's Word, and find a place to serve using your unique gifts.

Faith Kids

Our kids' ministry provides a safe, fun, and engaging environment for children from birth through 5th grade. Through age-appropriate lessons, worship, and activities, we partner with parents to build a strong spiritual foundation in their children's lives.

Learn More →

FAITH.YTH

Our youth group (6th-12th grade) is a dynamic community where students can navigate their teenage years with a biblical worldview. We focus on discipleship, building strong friendships, and having a lot of fun through weekly gatherings, retreats, and service projects.

Learn More →

Adult Ministries

We offer a variety of ways for adults to connect, including Community Groups, Men's and Women's Bible studies, and various fellowship events. These groups provide opportunities for deeper study, prayer, and building life-long, Christ-centered relationships.

Missions & Outreach

We are committed to sharing the gospel in our city and around the world. We partner with local organizations to serve our community and support missionaries who are taking the message of Jesus to the nations. Find out how you can get involved.
