Night to Shine

What is Night to Shine?

Night to Shine is a complimentary event for people with special needs hosted by local churches around the world, sponsored by the Tim Tebow Foundation. The event is open to anyone living with disabilities, ages 14 and older.

Each event is unique to its location, but some cornerstone activities include a red carpet entrance with paparazzi, hair and makeup stations, shoeshines, limousine rides, karaoke, gifts, a catered dinner, a Sensory Room, a Respite Room for parents and caregivers, dancing, and a crowning ceremony where every guest is honored as a King or a Queen - the way God sees them each and every day.

Information for Night to Shine 2026 will be coming in the fall.
