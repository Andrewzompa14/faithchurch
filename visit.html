We Can't Wait to Meet You

Visiting a new church for the first time can be intimidating, but we want to make your experience at Faith Church a great one. From the moment you walk in the door, we hope you'll feel right at home. Below you'll find everything you need to know to plan your visit.

What to Expect

THE SERVICE

Our services last about 75 minutes and include a time of worship through music and a message from the Bible that is relevant to your life.

WHAT TO WEAR

There's no dress code at Faith Church. You'll see everything from suits to jeans and t-shirts. Wear whatever makes you feel comfortable.

WHAT ABOUT KIDS?

We have a safe, fun, and engaging kids' ministry for children from birth to 5th grade. Your kids will love it!
