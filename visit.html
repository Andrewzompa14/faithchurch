Welcome to Faith Church

Faith Church is a place where people can meet Jesus, engage in life-giving community, and everyone is welcome. We believe in creating a space where people can have authentic encounters with Christ, discover their gifts, and use them for God's glory. Join us for our Sunday services!

What You Can Expect

The Service

A typical service will last about 75 minutes. We sing a mix of contemporary and traditional music led by our worship team. Childcare is available for babies through pre-school, and Children's Church (K-4th grade) starts mid-way through the service. As our guest, you are not expected to give during the offering.

Our Teaching

Messages at Faith Church are from the Bible and presented in a way that is relevant to everyday life. We believe the Bible is Godâ€™s living Word, and it comes alive when we apply it to our lives. Verses are projected on the screen to help you follow along.

Parking & Entrances

Parking is available all around the building. For your convenience, we have designated visitor parking spots on the lower level, which is the same level as our nursery and children's rooms. Entrances on the upper level are on the same level as the main worship center. Both levels are handicapped accessible, but there is no elevator between them.
