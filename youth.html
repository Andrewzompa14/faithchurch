WE ARE FOR THE NEXT GENERATION

FAITH.YTH is the student ministry of Faith Church for all students in 6th-12th grade. We exist to see students saved, healed, set free, discipled, equipped, empowered, and serving. We are a community that is passionate about Jesus and having a ton of fun along the way.

WHAT WE DO

YTH NIGHTS

Every Friday is a party! Join us for live worship, a relevant message, and time to hang out with friends. It's the best night of the week.

SMALL GROUPS

On Sunday mornings, we break into small groups to build relationships, ask tough questions, and dive deeper into God's Word together.

EVENTS

From summer camp and retreats to game nights and service projects, we always have something exciting happening. Check the calendar for what's next!
